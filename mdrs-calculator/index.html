Tier floor and CCD promoter rules (not configurable)

  • Irreversibility floor: CIS = 9 or 10 → minimum tier HIGH; CIS = 7 or 8 → minimum tier MEDIUM.
  • CCD promoter: CCD ≥ 8 promotes the resulting tier by one level (capped at CRITICAL).
  • These rules implement the paper's central novel contribution and are not subject to weight tuning.

4. Methodology

The composite

The Medical Device Risk Score composite is a weighted sum across five dimensions, each scored 1–10:

MDRS_comp = (CIS × 0.35) + (ES × 0.25) + (DCI × 0.20) + (NEF × 0.15) + (CCD × 0.05)

Scoring guidance

Refer to paper Table 5 for full scoring criteria per dimension. Brief reminders:

  • CIS (Clinical Impact): 9–10 life-sustaining; 7–8 critical monitoring; 5–6 diagnostic imaging; 3–4 administrative; 1–2 non-clinical.
  • ES (Exploitability): 9–10 network-accessible with public exploit; 5–6 adjacent-network or physical proximity; 1–2 privileged physical access only.
  • DCI (Criticality): 9–10 single point of failure; 5–6 redundant with <15 min switchover; 1–4 manual workaround.
  • NEF (Exposure): 9–10 internet-facing or exposed unprotected service port; 5–6 VLAN-isolated with permissive ACLs; 1–2 air-gapped.
  • CCD (Compensating Control Deficit): 9–10 no controls; 5–6 controls in 3–4 STRIDE categories; 1–2 comprehensive validated.
